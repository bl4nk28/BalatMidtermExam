Nuvali

Nature & Adventure Park

Escape the hustle and bustle of the city and immerse yourself in the beauty of nature at Nuvali. Enjoy biking, kayaking, and picnicking amidst scenic landscapes.

Solenad

Shopping & Dining Destination

Shop till you drop and indulge in delicious cuisines at Solenad, a premier lifestyle destination in Santa Rosa. With a variety of shops and restaurants, it's a shopper's paradise.

Miramonte Resort

Resort & Spa

Relax and rejuvenate at Miramonte Resort, a luxurious retreat nestled in the heart of Santa Rosa. Enjoy world-class amenities, serene
surroundings, and pampering spa treatments.

Santa Elena Golf & Country Club

Golf Course & Country Club

Hit the greens and enjoy a round of golf at Santa Elena Golf & Country Club. With its challenging course and
beautiful scenery, it's a favorite destination for golf
enthusiasts.

Sta. Rosa Museum

Cultural & Historical Landmark

Discover the rich history and heritage of Santa Rosa at Sta. Rosa Museum. Explore exhibits showcasing artifacts,
photographs, and memorabilia that tell the story of the city's past.

Sky Ranch

Family Entertainment Complex

Experience thrilling rides and exciting attractions at Sky Ranch, an amusement park offering fun for the whole family. From the iconic Sky Eye Ferris wheel to exhilarating rides like the Sky Cruiser and the Drop Tower, there's something for everyone. Enjoy delicious food and explore souvenir shops for a memorable day out.
